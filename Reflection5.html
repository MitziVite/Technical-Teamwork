Why is this week's topic important for teamwork? (20 points)

Professionalism is essential for teamwork because it creates a better environment for everyone. If we, do it, we can trust each other and be more productive. When we act with professionalism, people will depend more on us because they will know that we treat others with respect. Also, we take care of the company, arrive on time, keep our word, and don't gossip.
